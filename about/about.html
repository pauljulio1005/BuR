Warranty Policy

At Buy and Repair.net, we are committed to providing you with high-quality products and exceptional service, and we offer a comprehensive warranty policy to ensure your peace of mind with every purchase and repair.
All products purchased from us come with a standard 1-year warranty, though specific items may have different durations.
This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. If a product malfunctions or fails due to a manufacturing defect within the warranty period,
we will repair, replace, or refund the item based on our assessment.
However, the warranty does not cover damage caused by misuse, accidents, unauthorized repairs, wear and tear,
or environmental factors such as liquid exposure or extreme temperatures.
If you need to file a warranty claim, simply contact our customer service team with proof of purchase, and we will guide you through the process.

For repairs, we offer a 90-day warranty on all services provided. This covers the quality of our workmanship and any parts replaced during the repair.
If the same issue arises or the replacement part fails within 90 days, we will repair the device free of charge.
However, the warranty does not cover damage resulting from misuse, accidents, or issues unrelated to the original repair.
To claim a repair warranty, please reach out to our customer service team, and we will arrange for the necessary assessment and, if eligible, provide free repair services.

There are certain exclusions to our warranty coverage, including consumables like batteries, chargers,
screen protectors, and accessories unless they are defective upon arrival.
The warranty is valid only for the original purchaser and is not transferable.
Additionally, our warranty applies solely to products and repairs performed at Buy and Repair.net,
so please contact us for guidance if you require warranty service outside of the country of purchase.

To initiate a warranty claim for either a product or repair, please contact our support team with proof of purchase and a description of the issue.
If necessary, we may request that you return the product or repaired device for inspection.
Our team will then assess the issue and determine whether it qualifies for warranty service.
If approved, we will proceed with a repair, replacement, or refund. At Buy and Repair.net,
we strive to offer transparency and exceptional service, and we are dedicated to ensuring your satisfaction with our products and services.
Feel free to contact us with any questions regarding our warranty policy or to initiate a claim.
